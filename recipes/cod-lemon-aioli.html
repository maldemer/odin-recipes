Pan-seared Cod with Lemon Ailoi

Description

This lovely pan-seared cod with an easy,
homemade lemon aioli cooks exceptionally fast.
Pair with oven roasted vegetables and couscous for a fast,
but filling weeknight meal.

Ingredients

Lemon Aioli

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon finely grated garlic
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon milk, or more as needed

Cod

  • 4 (6 ounce) cod filets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
