Oven Fried Chicken



Image from All Recipes

Simple oven-fried breaded chicken.
Cut down on oil by making "fried" chicken in the oven.
This oven-fried recipe, made with simple ingredients is as good as it gets.


Prep time: 15 mins
Cook time: 1 hr
Total time: 1 hr 15 mins
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken thighs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place the flour in a shallow plate or bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    Put bread crumbs in another shallow plate or bowl and beat eggs in another bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken piece by piece in the flour, then the egg, then the bread crumbs,
    until all the pieces are coated.
  4. Pour oil into a large baking dish; add the breaded chicken thighs and
    sprinkle with paprika to taste.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, then turn over and bake for another 30 minutes.
    Remove from the oven and drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
